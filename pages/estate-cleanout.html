Estate Cleanout Services in Arlington, TX

Arlington Junk Removal provides compassionate, thorough estate cleanout services for families dealing with inherited properties, downsizing, or the loss of a loved one. We handle everything with care and respect.

We understand this is difficult

Estate cleanouts are different from regular junk removal. There's often an emotional weight to clearing out a home that belonged to someone you loved. We get that, and we don't treat these jobs like just another haul. We work at your pace, let you decide what stays and what goes, and handle the rest with respect. If you need to stop and take a break, that's fine. We're here to make a hard process easier.

What an estate cleanout includes

A full estate cleanout means clearing the entire contents of a home — furniture, appliances, clothing, kitchenware, personal items, garage contents, attic and storage items, and everything in between. We sort through items on-site. Anything you want to keep, we set aside. Usable items in good condition are donated to local Arlington charities. Recyclables are recycled. The rest is disposed of properly. When we leave, the home is empty, swept, and ready for whatever comes next — whether that's a sale, rental, or renovation.

Estate cleanout situations we handle

We work with families clearing out a home after the passing of a parent or loved one, people downsizing from a larger home to a smaller one, property managers clearing out rental properties after tenants leave, real estate agents preparing homes for listing, and families dealing with hoarding situations that require a careful, non-judgmental approach.

Pricing for estate cleanouts

Estate cleanouts are typically larger jobs. A small home or partial cleanout might be one to two full trailer loads, while a larger home with years of accumulated belongings can require multiple trips. We quote every estate cleanout on-site after walking through the property with you. Pricing depends on the volume of items, the number of rooms, and any special considerations like stairs or tight access. We give you a complete price upfront with no surprises.

Working with realtors and property managers

If you're a realtor or property manager in the Arlington area and need an estate cleared for listing or turnover, we can work on your timeline. We've handled foreclosure cleanouts, eviction cleanouts, and pre-sale property cleanups. Call us to discuss your specific situation and we'll put together a plan that fits your schedule.
