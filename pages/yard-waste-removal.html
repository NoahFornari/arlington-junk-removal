How yard waste removal works

Send us a photo of what needs to go via text at (817) 705-6612 or describe it over the phone. We'll give you a ballpark estimate. When we arrive, we walk the yard with you, confirm everything that needs to go, and give you a final all-inclusive price. Then we load it all up, rake or sweep the area, and haul everything to proper disposal or composting facilities.

Yard waste pricing

Yard waste is priced by volume like all our services. A small pile of branches or a few bags of leaves typically falls in the $150–$250 quarter-load range. Larger jobs like full yard cleanups, multiple tree limbs, or storm debris removal scale up from there. Heavy materials like dirt, rocks, or tree stumps may affect pricing due to weight. We always quote on-site before starting.

Why not just use city yard waste pickup?

Arlington's city brush and bulky item collection has specific rules — items must be placed curbside, brush must be cut to certain lengths, and there are pickup schedule limitations. If you've got a large amount of yard waste, missed the pickup window, or just want it gone today, we're the faster option. We come to wherever the debris is on your property and handle everything — no dragging it to the curb yourself.
