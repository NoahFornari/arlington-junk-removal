Residential Junk Removal in Arlington, TX

Arlington Junk Removal handles full-service residential junk removal for homes across Arlington and the DFW metroplex. We remove furniture, appliances, mattresses, electronics, and household clutter. Same-day service available.

How residential junk removal works

The process is simple. Call or text us at (817) 705-6612 with a description or photo of what you need removed. We'll give you a ballpark estimate over the phone. Then we schedule a time that works for you — often same-day or next-day.

When we arrive, we look at everything in person and give you a final all-inclusive price before we touch anything. No hidden fees, no surprises. Once you approve, we handle all the lifting, loading, and cleanup. We sweep the area when we're done and dispose of everything responsibly — donating usable items to local Arlington charities and recycling what we can.

Residential junk removal pricing

We charge by volume, not by the hour. A quarter trailer load (a few items like a couch and some boxes) starts around $120–$250. A half load (a room's worth of stuff) runs $250–$400. Three-quarter and full loads for larger cleanouts range from $400–$900. These are the most common residential price ranges in Arlington.

The type and weight of items can affect the final price — heavy items like concrete or full appliances may cost a bit more. But we always quote on-site before starting, so you know exactly what you're paying.

When to call for residential junk removal

Most Arlington homeowners call us when they're moving and need to clear out items they're not taking with them, decluttering rooms that have accumulated too much stuff over the years, dealing with a garage or attic that's completely full, replacing furniture or appliances and need the old ones gone, or cleaning up after a renovation or remodel.

There's no job too small. We'll come out for a single mattress or a single piece of furniture if that's all you need. And there's no job too big — we can handle whole-house cleanouts with multiple trailer loads if necessary.
