Garage Cleanout in Arlington, TX

Arlington Junk Removal clears out cluttered garages across Arlington and the DFW area. We remove everything you don't want, haul it away, and leave you with a usable space again.

Reclaim your garage

If your garage has turned into a storage unit you can't park in anymore, you're not alone — it's one of the most common calls we get. Years of accumulated stuff piles up until the garage is completely unusable. We come in, help you sort through everything, remove what you don't want, and leave you with an actual garage again.

What we remove from garages

Everything. Old furniture that got moved out of the house but never made it to the dump. Broken appliances waiting to be fixed that never got fixed. Boxes of stuff from the last two moves. Old paint cans, holiday decorations you'll never use again, kids' toys they outgrew years ago, exercise equipment collecting dust, scrap wood, old tools, bikes with flat tires — all of it. We take everything except hazardous materials like chemicals, fuel, and pressurized containers.

How garage cleanouts work

We show up, look at everything in the garage, and give you a price based on volume. Then we start loading. If there are items you want to keep, just point them out and we'll work around them. We load everything else into the trailer, sweep the garage floor, and you're done. Most single-garage cleanouts take one to two hours.

Garage cleanout pricing

A typical garage cleanout in Arlington falls in the half-load to full-load range depending on how packed the garage is. That's roughly $250–$900. If your garage is moderately cluttered, expect the $400–$600 range. We quote on-site and you approve before we start.
