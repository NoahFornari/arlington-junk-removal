About Arlington Junk Removal

Locally owned and operated junk removal in Arlington, TX. We started this business to give Arlington and the DFW metroplex honest, affordable, and reliable junk hauling service.

Why we started Arlington Junk Removal

We saw too many national franchises overcharging and providing impersonal service to Arlington homeowners. A couch removal shouldn't cost $400. A garage cleanout shouldn't require three phone calls to a call center in another state. We knew we could do better because we actually live here.

Arlington Junk Removal was built on a simple idea: show up when we say we will, charge a fair price, do the job right, and clean up after ourselves. That's it. No franchise fees inflating your bill. No corporate scripts. Just a local crew that treats your home with respect and gets the job done.

What we stand for

Honest, upfront pricing

We price by volume — how much space your stuff takes in our trailer. We quote on-site before we touch anything, and the price we quote is the price you pay. No hidden fees, no hourly rates, no "oh, that item costs extra" surprises. Our pricing ranges from $150 for a quarter load to $900 for a full trailer.

Eco-friendly disposal

We don't just dump everything in a landfill. Usable furniture, clothing, and household goods go to local Arlington charities and churches. Recyclable materials like metal, cardboard, and electronics go to appropriate recycling facilities. We sort through every load to divert as much as possible from the landfill. It's the right thing to do.

Respect for your property

We're careful with your home. We protect doorways and floors when carrying items out. We don't drag furniture across hardwood. We sweep the area when we're done. Your home should look better when we leave, not worse.

Local knowledge

We know Arlington. From the neighborhoods near AT&T Stadium and Globe Life Field to the residential areas of South Arlington, we know the streets, the neighborhoods, and the best disposal and donation options. Being local means we can get to you faster and serve you better than any national chain.
